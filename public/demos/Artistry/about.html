About the Artist

As a contemporary artist, I explore the intersection between emotion and form. My works are inspired by the subtle beauty of everyday moments, interpreted through a minimalist perspective that invites contemplation.

I primarily work with oil, acrylic, and mixed media techniques. I create pieces that balance spontaneity with deliberate restraint. Each artwork is an exploration of texture, color, and the quiet power of negative space.

My works have been exhibited in galleries across the country and are part of international private collections. I currently maintain my studio in Toronto, continuing to push the boundaries of my art.
